Boiled egg Recipe

Description

Use this recipe for perfectly cooked hard-boiled eggs. By adding a little vinegar and salt, the eggshell peels off so easily without tearing or sticking. I have been making them this way for years!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • White vinegar
  • Water
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients (excluding egg) in a large pot and wait for the water to boil.
  2. Add the egg slowly and reduce the heat to a gentle boil. Cook for 14 minutes.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  4. Once eggs have cooked, remove them from the hot water, and place into a container of ice water or cold, running water. Cool completely, about 15 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
